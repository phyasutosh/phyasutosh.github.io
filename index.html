Welcome to our family of physicists

The Department of Physics, Asutosh college have a legacy of nurturing young minds backed with a vibrant and dynamic group of world-renowned physicists. This department carries the legacy of stalwarts like Prof. Amal Roychowdhury, Prof. K.G. Majumdar and many more. We offer a 3-year undergraduate curriculum offered by University of Calcutta and a 2-year postgraduate curriculum in distance mode offered by Vidyasagar University. We take pride of nurturing young minds both from theoretical classes, state-of-the-art laboratory equipments and innovative projects on experiment and numerical computation to progress in their respective career.
